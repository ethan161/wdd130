Site Name

Dry Oar Rafting Resources

Site Purpose with Goals

The purpose of the site is to allow customers to easily access the information they desire, prioritizing contact information and prices.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • I need to contact the equipment store.
  • I need to check their available stock to make sure they have my lifejacket size.
  • I would like to learn more about the area, including popular locations to raft.
  • Are their weekends that I could schedule a tour guide from the company?

Logo

Color Scheme

To stick with a similar color as the logo, I will opt for a saturated river-blue as the primary color, and orange as the secondary contrast color. The background will be an off-white color.

Typography to be Used

The majority of the website will be written with the font "Poppins" from the Google font website.
