Intended Target Audience

My target audience for this website will be aspiring or professional travelers that are searching for their next getaway destination. There are many age groups that work with this demographic, but I will be specifically targeting an older generation that is not as familiar with technology by making this site as simple and readable as possible.

Color Scheme

My color scheme here will attempt to match the vibrant and contrasting colors of the Micronesian islands, with the colors:

  • Deep Sky Blue - #0160D6
  • Surf Blue – #00AAF8
  • Orange Red – #F13B13
  • Lawn Green – #AAB624
  • Faint Gray – #F0ECE9 (This is the page background color)

Typography

Similar to my text, I wanted something that matched the islands that I will be promoting: warm, inviting, and easy on the eyes. I have chosen the 'Lato' family from the Google fonts website, of which this page is written in.

Part 2: Sitemap Content

Main page

This page will be dedicated to general information, island statistics, and pictures. This main page will mostly be dedicated to piqueing the interest of potential tourists. Users can expect to see exclusive pictures and behind-the-scenes contentthat will not be on other websites.

Local Attraction Spots

Because there is not a lot of information about the Micronesian islands out there, this page will be dedicated to showcasing the best tourist locations, including the ones that are not on maps or websites. This page will include maps and directions.

Cultural Highlights

For those that are traveling to meet new people and see different ways of life, this page will be all about the people of Pohnpei. Basic language will be introduced here as well as things to expect as a foreign traveler.
