Attractions

Hikes are a staple to Pohnpei's natural beauty. Over 80% of the landmass has no connecting road, meaning if you want to catch this island in all its splendor, it might require some walking.

Pohnpei's coral reef is home to many exotic animals, including manta rays. Pohnpei is world-renowned for its exclusive and stunning underwater landscapes.

Nan Madol is Pohnpei's biggest unsolved mystery. These ruins have been untouched for years, but recently locals have allowed tourists to hike in and interact with these buildings.
