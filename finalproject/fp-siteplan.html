Intended Target Audience

My target audience for this website will be aspiring or professional travelers that are searching for their next getaway destination. There are many age groups that work with this demographic, but I will be specifically targeting an older generation that is not as familiar with technology by making this site as simple and readable as possible.

Color Scheme

My color scheme here will attempt to match the vibrant and contrasting colors of the Micronesian islands, with the colors:

  • Deep Sky Blue - #0160D6
  • Surf Blue – #00AAF8
  • Orange Red – #F13B13
  • Lawn Green – #AAB624
  • Faint Gray – #F0ECE9 (This is the page background color)

Typography

Similar to my text, I wanted something that matched the islands that I will be promoting: warm, inviting, and easy on the eyes. I have chosen the 'Lato' family from the Google fonts website, of which this page is written in.
