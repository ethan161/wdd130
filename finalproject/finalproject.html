A Traveler's Getaway

Hidden in the depths of the Pacific is the most laid back island you've never heard of. Home to the second rainiest climate on Earth, Pohnpei is the capital of the Federated States of Micronesia that over 40,000 people call home.

Solitude

Dubbed as the least visited country in the world, the last thing you need to do is worry about long lines or packed buildings. Pohnpei has activities that can satisfy the resort lounger and the adventurous. No need to worry about currency either, as Pohnpei uses the US dollar.

Diving

Although Pohnpei might not boast the most luxurious of activites, its diving course has been ranked as one of the most relaxed and untouched. Even the beginner scuba diver can appreciate Pohnpei's tranquil bays.

Tourism

With its biggest commodity being tourism, Pohnpei prides itself on its scuba diving, comfortable resorts, and stunning hikes.
